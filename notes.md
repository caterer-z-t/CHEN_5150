## rate forms

- mass action kinetics $rate = k[a][b]$
- saturating/hyperbolic $ \frac{rate}{rate_{mak}} = \frac{[A]/k}{1+[a]/k}$
- cooperative $\frac{rate}{rate_{max}}=\frac{([A]/k)^n}{1+([a]/k)^n}$

## Kinetic rate constants & dissociation constants
$P + L <->^{k_{on}}_{k_{off}} C$

rate of complex formation = $k_{on}[P][L]$

rate of complex dissociation = $k_{off}[C]$

at steady state $k_{on}[P][L] = k_{off}[C]$

eq: $k_{on}[P]_{eq}[L]_{eq} = k_{off}[C]_{eq} \implies \frac{[P]_{eq}[L]_{eq}}{[C]_{eq}} = \frac{k_{off}}{k_{on}} \equiv k_j$
